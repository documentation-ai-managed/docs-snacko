## Quick Start

1. Sign up at `https://app.snacko.com`.
2. Choose to use Snacko for yourself or as a dietitian.
3. Set up your profile:
   - Personal: Add your nutrition goals.
   - Dietitian: Create your practice and add clients.
4. Start tracking meals or create diet plans.
5. Access progress dashboards anytime.

Snacko guides you through setup with simple prompts. You can edit your preferences and goals anytime from the settings panel.
