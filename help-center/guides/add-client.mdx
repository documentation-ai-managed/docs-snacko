## Add Client in Snacko

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Clients">
    From the dashboard, click on "Clients" in the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite New Client">
    Click "Add Client" and enter their email and basic info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Diet Plan">
    After client accepts the invite, assign or create a personalized diet plan for them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Clients will be notified and can start tracking as soon as they join.
