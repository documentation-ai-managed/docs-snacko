## Before You Begin

<Callout kind="info">

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A valid email address for account verification
- Access to a modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- Your dietary goals in mind (e.g., weight loss, muscle gain, or balanced nutrition)

Snacko works for both personal users tracking their own intake and dietitians managing clients.

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and get verified.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Snacko and Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

    Navigate to `https://app.snacko.com/signup`.

    Fill in your details:

    | Field       | Example Value              |
    |-------------|----------------------------|
    | Email       | yourname@example.com      |
    | Password    | (Choose a strong password) |
    | User Type   | Personal or Dietitian     |

    Click **Create Account**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">

    Check your inbox for a verification email from Snacko.

    Click the **Verify Email** button in the email.

    You'll be redirected to the dashboard upon success.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or request a new one from the login page.

</Callout>

## Navigate the Dashboard

Snacko's dashboard adapts to your user type. Use the sidebar to access key features like Meals, Goals, and Clients (for dietitians).

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Personal User" icon="user">

    - **Today**: Log meals and see daily progress.
    - **Goals**: Track macros, calories, and habits.
    - **History**: View past logs and trends.
    - **Reports**: Generate nutrition summaries.

    Quick stats show calories consumed vs. target, with progress bars for protein, carbs, and fats.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Dietitian" icon="users">

    - **Clients**: Add and manage client profiles.
    - **Plans**: Create custom diet plans.
    - **Monitoring**: View client logs and adherence.
    - **Insights**: Analyze group trends.

    Switch between clients using the top dropdown.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Log Your First Meal

Track intake effortlessly with Snacko's intuitive logger.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Meal Type" icon="utensils">

    From the dashboard, click **Log Meal**.

    Choose from Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snack.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Nutrition Details" icon="edit-3">

    Input your meal:

    - Food: `Grilled Chicken Salad`
    - Quantity: `300g`
    - Calories: `450`
    - Protein: `35g`
    - Carbs: `15g`
    - Fat: `25g`

    Or scan a barcode for auto-fill.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Review" icon="check-circle">

    Click **Save**. View it in **Today** with a breakdown.

    Edit anytime via the meal card.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Your Initial Goals

<Expandable title="Configure Goals" default-open="true">

Define targets to guide your tracking.

1. Go to **Goals** in the sidebar.
2. Set daily targets:
   - Calories: `2500`
   - Protein: `150g`
   - Carbs: `250g`
   - Fat: `80g`
3. Choose habit goals like `Drink 8 glasses of water` or `Eat veggies with every meal`.
4. Save. Snacko sends reminders and progress updates.

For dietitians, set client-specific goals from their profile.

</Expandable>

<Callout kind="success">

Congratulations! You've logged your first entry. Snacko uses behavior science to nudge healthier choices.

</Callout>
